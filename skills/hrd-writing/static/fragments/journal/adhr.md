# Journal: ADHR

Use for Advances in Developing Human Resources orientation.

Emphasize:

- HRD issue relevance for scholars and practitioners
- accessible but scholarly framing
- thematic, issue-focused, or practice-connected contribution
- implications that help HRD professionals act thoughtfully

Fit check:

- Is the practical relevance clear without weakening scholarly grounding?
- Are practitioner implications specific and evidence-aware?
- Does the manuscript speak to a recognizable HRD problem or theme?
