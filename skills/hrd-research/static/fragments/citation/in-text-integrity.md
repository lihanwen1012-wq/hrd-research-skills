# Citation: In-Text Integrity

Use with `static/core/citation-integrity.md`.

Check:

- each source-dependent claim has an adjacent citation
- citations move with claims during revision
- paragraph-final citations are not stretched across unrelated claims
- narrative and parenthetical citation forms are APA-consistent
- synthesized claims keep the correct sources attached

Flag:

- `[citation needed]`
- `[verify citation support]`
- `[citation too far from claim]`
- `[paragraph-final citation may be overextended]`
