# Journal: HRDR

Use for Human Resource Development Review orientation.

Emphasize:

- conceptual, theoretical, integrative, or critical contribution
- synthesis across bodies of literature
- clear argument architecture
- explicit advancement of HRD theory, assumptions, or research agenda

Fit check:

- Does the manuscript do more than summarize prior studies?
- Is the conceptual contribution named and defended?
- Are implications for future HRD inquiry clear?
