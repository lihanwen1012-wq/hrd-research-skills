# Journal: HRDQ

Use for Human Resource Development Quarterly orientation.

Emphasize:

- strong empirical or theory-informed contribution to HRD
- clear research design and evidence quality
- explicit contribution to HRD scholarship
- implications grounded in findings rather than generic practice advice

Fit check:

- Does the manuscript explain why the problem matters to HRD specifically?
- Are methods and claims aligned?
- Are theoretical and practical contributions both visible?
