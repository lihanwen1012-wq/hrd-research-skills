# Genre: Empirical

For quantitative, qualitative, mixed-methods, or evaluation studies.

Preserve:

- research design
- sample and context
- variables, constructs, measures, and analytic approach
- results and statistical or qualitative claims

Require cautious interpretation. Do not imply causality from correlational, cross-sectional, exploratory, or qualitative designs unless the user provides a defensible causal design.

Connect findings back to HRD theory, workforce learning, development, or performance practice.
