# Paper Development: Evidence Bank

Use this when building claims from data, literature notes, figures, tables, findings, or user materials.

Create or recommend a claim-evidence map:

| Claim | Evidence Provided | Citation Needed | Strength | Risk | Revision Note |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Rules:

- Treat user-provided materials as authoritative for this project.
- Do not invent evidence or citations.
- Mark weak claims as "needs evidence" or "needs citation."
- Separate empirical findings from interpretation and implications.
- Identify where a figure, table, or appendix would support the claim better than prose.

For HRD papers, make sure each major claim connects to HRD constructs, theory, context, or practice relevance.
